## Brand Identity

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Deep navy / dark blue (`#0A1628` or similar)
- **Secondary/Accent:** Electric blue / cyan (`#0066CC` or `#00A8E8`)
- **Neutral Light:** White (`#FFFFFF`) and off-white (`#F5F7FA`)
- **Text Dark:** Near-black (`#0D1B2A`)
- **Subtle Mid:** Light gray (`#E8EDF2`) for section dividers and cards
- Gradient transitions from **dark navy → white** across sections to create depth

### Typography
- **Headings:** A modern, geometric sans-serif — e.g., *Inter*, *DM Sans*, or *Neue Haas Grotesk*. Bold weight. Professional and clean.
- **Body:** Same family, regular weight. High legibility.
- **Accent / Taglines:** Slightly lighter weight, italics optional for emphasis
- No decorative or script fonts.

### Visual Style
- Minimalist and data-forward — think dashboards, clean grids, subtle data visualisations as background elements
- Dark hero sections fading into white/light content sections
- Subtle glowing blue accents on interactive elements (buttons, hover states)
- Abstract AI/data-themed background visuals (particle networks, flowing data lines, circuit-like patterns) — tasteful, not gimmicky
- Professional photography placeholder areas for team headshots
